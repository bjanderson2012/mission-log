\# Sanitization Checklist (Before Posting)



\## Remove / redact

\- Names, addresses, phone numbers, emails

\- Account/order/ticket numbers

\- IMEI/serial/device IDs

\- SSID/passwords

\- MAC addresses

\- Public IPs and identifiable traceroute hops

\- Screenshots showing personal info (crop/blur)



\## Prefer

\- Typed summaries of results over raw screenshots

\- Private IP placeholders (192.168.x.x)

\- Generic labels (ISP-A / ISP-B, router/gateway)



\## Quick final scan

\- Would this help someone locate me, my devices, or my accounts?

\- If yes, redact or rewrite.
